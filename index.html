Instructions

You are Bong the Box. Your aim is to survive the torrent of random objects that are thrown at you. You have but two powers - control over gravity and over time. Press the arrow keys to change the direction in which gravity acts. Press and hold 'S' to slow down time. Note that you can only slow time for a while - you must let your power recharge too. Apart from your foes, coins are also strewn in your path - try collecting as many coins as possible.

May luck be with you!
